About

Passion for technology, learning new things, and setting and achieving goals are some of the pillars in my career. Putting all my efforts into making my dreams come true is also very important to me, like when I graduated as an Electromechanical Engineer, a profession in which I worked for almost 3 years. This experience allowed me to provide technical, managerial and even commercial solutions according to each client's needs.

Nowadays, my enthusiasm for programming took me back to my origins as a student of Information Systems Engineering to fulfill the dream I had since I was young: to become a Software Developer. I started studying the basics of programming in Python, JavaScript, and TypeScript. With the clear objective of becoming a Full-Stack Developer, I began learning Angular, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL, sophisticated technologies that allow me to understand the development of a web application from Front-End to Back-End.

In order to face the different challenges this kind of work offers, one also has to know what technology is appropriate. For this reason, I am always willing to learn and adapt to other tools such as React, Vue.js, Node.js, and Django to mention a few.

I am very excited and eager to have the opportunity to work in software development teams with the determination, dedication, and commitment that define me!
